#+TITLE: Project Workspace

* Purpose

This workspace preserves non-authoritative discussions that help the project
converge. It may contain architecture questions, API/SPI trade-offs,
implementation-readiness proposals, development-process improvements, review
inboxes, experiments, and collaboration guidance.

Authoritative decisions are migrated into =docs/=, =AGENTS.md=, or another
explicit project-policy owner. Implementation status remains in =PLAN.md= and
the work-package DAG rather than in topic prose.

* Lifecycle

OPEN -> DISCUSSING -> DECIDED -> MIGRATED

- OPEN: A question or review finding has been identified.
- DISCUSSING: Alternatives and trade-offs are under active discussion.
- DECIDED: The direction has converged but is not yet projected where needed.
- MIGRATED: The stable conclusion is present in its authoritative owner.

Topics are retained after migration as decision history.
